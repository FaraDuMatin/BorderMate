## Overview

BorderMate provides a **fully automated translation pipeline** that:
1. **Listens** to your speech in real-time (Speech-to-Text)
2. **Translates** it automatically to the target language
3. **Speaks** the translation out loud (Text-to-Speech)

All of this happens in **under 2 seconds**, creating a natural conversation flow.

---

## Use Cases

- **International Business Meetings**: Real-time interpretation for cross-border teams
- **Customer Service**: Assist customers who speak different languages
- **Travel & Tourism**: Communicate with locals without language barriers
- **Healthcare**: Bridge communication gaps with non-native speakers
- **Education**: Help students and teachers communicate across languages
